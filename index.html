From the Sea to Your Table

PrimeCatch PH was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: bring the freshest seafood and premium meats directly to Filipino households — without the middlemen, without compromise.

We work hand-in-hand with local fishermen in Batangas, Cebu, and Palawan, as well as certified farms in Bataan and Nueva Ecija, ensuring every product meets our strict quality standards.

Our cold-chain logistics guarantee that what leaves the sea or farm in the morning arrives at your doorstep still at its peak — fresh, safe, and delicious.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you deliver to? +
We currently deliver to all cities within Metro Manila. We're expanding to Cavite, Laguna, and Bulacan soon!
How do you ensure freshness during delivery? +
All orders are packed in insulated boxes with ice gel packs to maintain temperature. Orders are also dispatched within 2 hours of confirmation.
What's your minimum order? +
There's no minimum order! Delivery fee is ₱150 for orders below ₱2,000, and FREE for orders ₱2,000 and above.
Can I schedule a specific delivery time? +
Yes! When checking out, you can choose morning (8AM–12PM) or afternoon (1PM–6PM) delivery slots.
Do you offer wholesale pricing? +
Absolutely. We cater to restaurants, hotels, and food businesses. Contact us at wholesale@primecatch.ph for wholesale rates.
